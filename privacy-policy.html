Schedule Flow Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 21, 2026

Your schedule data lives on your device. We don't run a cloud backend, and we don't require an account.

1. Information We Collect

Schedule Flow is built around a local-first architecture. The app stores your events, tasks, reminders, categories, and preferences directly on your device using a local SQLite database.

2. AI Assistant & Voice Input

When you use the AI chat or voice input feature, your message is sent to our AI service provider (DeepSeek) to generate a response. The data transmitted includes your schedule-related input (e.g., event titles, task descriptions, time information you provide) so that the AI can give relevant suggestions.

  • What we send: Your free-text input and schedule context — not your full database, only what you type.
  • What we don't do: We do not persistently store, log, or review your AI conversation content on any server we operate.
  • Data retention by DeepSeek: DeepSeek may process and temporarily retain your input to deliver the service. We do not control DeepSeek's data practices beyond what is described in their policy. Please review their privacy policy for details:
    • DeepSeek Privacy Policy
  • AI-generated content: Responses generated by the AI are for informational convenience only. Schedule Flow does not guarantee the accuracy, completeness, or reliability of AI outputs. All AI suggestions must be reviewed by you before being accepted into your schedule.
  • Prohibited uses: You agree not to use the AI feature to generate content that is unlawful, harmful, or deceptive.

3. Data Storage

  • All calendar, task, and reminder data is stored locally on your device.
  • We do not operate a server that stores your data.
  • Your data is not shared with us or third parties for analytics, advertising, or profiling.

4. Data Backup & Export

Schedule Flow allows you to export your data as an encrypted backup file (.sfbackup) and import it on the same or another device.

  • Encryption: Backup files are encrypted using AES-256-CBC with a password you choose. We do not store or transmit your password. If you lose your password, the backup cannot be recovered.
  • Export: When you export data, the app reads your local database, encrypts it, and shares the file through the system share sheet (e.g., AirDrop, Messages, Files). The file is only sent where you choose.
  • Import: When importing a backup, you can choose to overwrite existing data (all current data is replaced) or merge (backup data is added; conflicting records are replaced by the backup version). You will be asked to confirm before any data is modified.
  • Local only: Backup files are never uploaded to any server. All encryption and decryption happens on your device.

5. Third-Party Services

The only third-party service currently used is the AI chat provider (DeepSeek). We do not use analytics SDKs, advertising networks, or crash reporters.

If we add new third-party services in the future, this Privacy Policy will be updated and the relevant information will be provided.

6. California Privacy Rights (CCPA/CPRA)

If you are a California resident, you have specific rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA):

  • Right to Know: You may request disclosure of what personal information we collect, use, and share about you. Given our local-first architecture, most of your personal information (schedules, tasks, reminders) never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.
  • Right to Delete: You may request deletion of your personal information. Because your data is stored locally on your device, deleting the app also deletes your data from our reach. We do not hold copies of your schedule data on our servers.
  • Right to Correct: You may request correction of inaccurate personal information about you.
  • Right to Opt-Out of Sale/Sharing: We do not sell or share your personal information to third parties for advertising or profiling purposes. As such, there is nothing to opt out of.
  • Right to Limit Use of Sensitive Personal Information: We do not collect sensitive personal information (e.g., SSN, precise location, health data) as defined under CPRA.
  • No Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising any of your privacy rights.

To exercise any of the above rights, or if you have questions, contact us at: mel91229@163.com. We will respond within the time required by applicable law.

7. Children's Privacy

Schedule Flow is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the revised policy.

9. Pricing & Subscriptions

Schedule Flow uses a freemium model:

  • Free Tier: The free version includes unlimited AI queries and up to 10 AI-assisted edits per day (adding, editing, or deleting schedules and tasks). AI queries (searching and reading your schedule) are always unlimited.
  • Premium Plans: Paid options (including one-time purchase and subscription) will be launched in the future to remove the daily edit limit and unlock additional premium features.
  • Pricing & Availability: Specific pricing, subscription tiers, and launch date for the paid plan will be communicated through the App Store product page and in-app notices when available.
  • In-App Purchases: All paid features, if any, will be processed through Apple's In-App Purchase system. We do not redirect users to external payment methods.
  • Cancellation: You can manage or cancel your subscription at any time through your Apple ID account settings (Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions). Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period.
