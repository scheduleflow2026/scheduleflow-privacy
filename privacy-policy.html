Schedule Flow Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 20, 2026

Your schedule data lives on your device. We don't run a cloud backend, and we don't require an account.

1. Information We Collect

Schedule Flow is built around a local-first architecture. The app stores your events, tasks, reminders, categories, and preferences directly on your device using a local SQLite database.

2. AI Assistant & Voice Input

When you use the AI chat or voice input feature, your message is sent to our AI service provider (DeepSeek) to generate a response. We do not store or log these conversations on any backend operated by us. Please refer to the AI provider's privacy policy for details on how they process your input:

  • DeepSeek Privacy Policy

3. Data Storage

  • All calendar, task, and reminder data is stored locally on your device.
  • We do not operate a server that stores your data.
  • Your data is not shared with us or third parties for analytics, advertising, or profiling.

4. Third-Party Services

The only third-party service currently used is the AI chat provider. We do not use analytics SDKs, advertising networks, or crash reporters.

5. Children's Privacy

Schedule Flow is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the revised policy.

7. Pricing & Subscriptions

Schedule Flow uses a freemium model:

  • Free Tier: The free version includes unlimited AI queries and up to 10 AI-assisted edits per day (adding, editing, or deleting schedules and tasks). AI queries (searching and reading your schedule) are always unlimited.
  • Premium Plans: Paid options (including one-time purchase and subscription) will be launched in the future to remove the daily edit limit and unlock additional premium features.
  • Pricing & Availability: Specific pricing, subscription tiers, and launch date for the paid plan will be communicated through the App Store product page and in-app notices when available.
  • In-App Purchases: All paid features, if any, will be processed through Apple's In-App Purchase system. We do not redirect users to external payment methods.
  • Cancellation: You can manage or cancel your subscription at any time through your Apple ID account settings (Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions). Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period.
