ScheduleFlow Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 29, 2026

At a glance: ScheduleFlow is a 100% local-storage app. Your schedules, tasks, and personal data never leave your device unless you explicitly use the AI assistant, which sends your input to DeepSeek for processing only. We do not collect, sell, or share your personal information with any party.

1 Information We Do NOT Collect

ScheduleFlow is designed to keep your data on your device. We do not collect, store, transmit, or have access to any of the following:

  • Your name, email, phone number, or any contact information
  • Account credentials (no account required to use the app)
  • Your schedules, tasks, or calendar data (all stored locally on your device)
  • Precise location data (GPS) — though the app may determine your time zone from your device's system settings
  • Device identifiers (IDFA, advertising IDs, etc.)
  • Usage analytics, behavioral data, or clickstream tracking
  • Crash reports or diagnostic data (we do not use any third-party crash reporting service)
  • Photos, contacts, or any data from other apps on your device

2 Data Stored on Your Device

The following information is stored only on your device (local SQLite database) and is never transmitted to us or any server:

  • Schedules and tasks you create
  • Custom categories, colors, and icons
  • Reminders and notification settings
  • App preferences (language, time zone, time format)
Your control: You can permanently delete all local data at any time by uninstalling the app, or by using "Clear All Data" in the Profile screen. We have no ability to recover deleted data.

3 AI Assistant — Third-Party Processing

ScheduleFlow includes an optional AI assistant that helps you create, edit, and query schedules using natural language. The feature is powered by DeepSeek, a third-party AI service operated by Beijing DeepSeek AI Technology Co., Ltd.

When you use AI features, the following data is sent to DeepSeek:

  • Your natural language input (e.g., "schedule a meeting tomorrow at 3pm", "what's on my calendar next week?")
  • Minimal context needed to understand your request — for example, if you ask about your existing events, the relevant event details will be included so the AI can answer your question

What is NOT sent to DeepSeek:

  • Your account information (no account required)
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Data you haven't explicitly asked the AI to process
⚠️ International Data Transfer Disclosure:
The AI assistant is powered by DeepSeek (Beijing DeepSeek AI Technology Co., Ltd.), a company incorporated in the People's Republic of China. When you use the AI assistant, your input is transmitted to and processed on DeepSeek's servers, which may be located in China or other countries. Under applicable Chinese law, DeepSeek may be subject to legal obligations that could require disclosure of certain data to authorities. This is a characteristic of using a Chinese AI service, not a special or unusual practice. We encourage you to review DeepSeek's Privacy Policy before using the AI assistant.

DeepSeek's data policy: DeepSeek states it processes your input in real-time and does not use it to train or improve its models. Data is handled in accordance with DeepSeek's Privacy Policy.

Free usage limit: Free users are limited to 10 AI create/modify/delete operations per day (query operations are not counted). The usage count is stored locally on your device only and resets at midnight in your device's local time zone. It is not transmitted to or stored on any server. Subscribers may have this limit removed. Relevant terms will be announced when the subscription service launches.

You can use ScheduleFlow fully offline — all core features (calendar, tasks, reminders, backups, category management) work without any internet connection and do not involve any data transmission.

4 Encrypted Backup & Restore

ScheduleFlow lets you export your data as an encrypted backup file (.sfbackup). The backup is encrypted with AES-256-GCM using a key derived from a password you choose (PBKDF2).

The backup file is generated and decrypted entirely on your device. It is stored only where you choose to save it (Files app, iCloud Drive, etc.). We do not have access to your backup file, your password, or your decrypted data.

5 Children's Privacy (COPPA & GDPR)

ScheduleFlow is intended for general audiences and is not directed to children under 13. Because we do not collect any personal information on our servers — your data stays entirely on your device — there is no personal information belonging to children (or anyone else) that we could inadvertently collect or retain.

The only data that leaves your device is the AI input you voluntarily submit (see Section 3). If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has used the AI assistant to submit personal information, please contact us at mel91229@163.com and we will direct you to DeepSeek's data deletion procedures.

6 Your Rights — CCPA (California)

If you are a California resident, under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information is collected about you
  • Delete your personal information
  • Opt out of the sale of your personal information (we do not sell data)
  • Non-discrimination — we will not discriminate against you for exercising your rights

Since we do not collect or hold any personal information on our servers, there is no data held by us to disclose, correct, or delete. All your data — schedules, tasks, categories, preferences — resides solely on your device, under your exclusive control. To delete your data, you can use the "Clear All Data" function in the Profile screen, or simply uninstall the App.

To exercise your rights: Contact us at mel91229@163.com. We will respond within 45 days.

7 Your Rights — GDPR (European Economic Area)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) applies to our processing of your personal data — specifically, the AI input data transmitted to DeepSeek (see Section 3). We do not collect or hold any personal data on our own servers.

Because we do not hold server-side data, rights relating to data we control (access, rectification, erasure, portability of server-side data) are not applicable to us. You retain the right to lodge a complaint (Art. 77) with your local supervisory authority at any time, without needing to contact us first. For data subject rights relating to AI processing by DeepSeek, please refer to DeepSeek's Privacy Policy.

Note on AI data transfers: Your AI assistant inputs are transferred to DeepSeek (a Chinese company) for processing. This constitutes an international data transfer under GDPR. The European Commission has not issued an adequacy decision for China. By using the AI assistant, you acknowledge this transfer. All other app features involve no personal data transfer outside your device.

8 Data Security

We implement the following technical and organizational measures to protect your local data:

  • All app data stored in the iOS sandbox (not accessible to other apps)
  • Encrypted backup files use AES-256-GCM with user-controlled passwords
  • No data transmitted over the network except AI assistant inputs (optional, user-initiated)
  • AI inputs transmitted only over HTTPS to DeepSeek's API endpoint

No security measure is 100% impenetrable. We cannot guarantee the security of data stored on your device or transmitted over the internet, and you acknowledge this inherent risk.

If the changes involve how we handle AI assistant data or introduce new third-party integrations, we will provide prominent notice before the changes take effect.

Your continued use of ScheduleFlow after changes take effect constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
